Star Wars fans are impossible to please. I'd know, I'm one of them.

Everybody's entitled to their own opinions. I'm also entitled to judge those opinions with no remorse. I usually keep to myself, but when I see that someone that unironically thinks The Phantom Menace is a masterpiece, then I can't hold my silence.

Here's my entirely biased ranking of the nine Star Wars movies.

  1. Empire Strikes Back
  2. A New Hope
  3. The Force Awakens
  4. Return of the Jedi
  5. The Last Jedi
  6. Revenge of the Sith
  7. The Rise of Skywalker
  8. Attack of the Clones
  9. The Phantom Menace

Admittedly, the bottom three films were difficult to rank. They all have their own problems, but if I had to watch one, it would be The Rise of Skywalker.
