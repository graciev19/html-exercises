The year 2021 was a hard year for everyone both mentally and physicall, espceically for us students who needed to relive zoom university for the second year in a row. That is why for the new year of 2022, here are 3 tips that will motviate and prepare you for a succesful school year!

How to Suceed in School Post-Pandemic

Step 1: Get a good nights rest...seariously!

  • Studies have shown that those who make effort to get about 7 to 8 hours of sleep wake up feeling energized and have brain activity that works faster than when you are tired. It's natural that sometimes sleeping to much or too little than your normal hours will make you more tired. But practice makes perfect right?
  • Set an alarm clock a couple minutes to an hour prior to going to bed. This way, you cna track how much time you would need to complete assignments and prepare for bed.
  • Manage your workload by beginning your homework at the beginning of the day. That way, you wouldn't need to stay up till dawn completing assignments.
Tip: Grab a friend or two to help you put these tips into practice, and monitor how each of your sleep has improved.

Step 2: Take a break

  • Use this time to refelct on the remaining things you would want to do that is fun later in the day.
  • Take a walk around your neigborhood or do some stretches to get your body moving.
  • Listen to some soothing music that will calm the mind and body, using positive energy
Tip: Be mindful of what your brain and body can take. Don't force yourself beyond what you've already accomplished. Rather, be proud of yourself!
