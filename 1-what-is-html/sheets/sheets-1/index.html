Rating and Reviewing Some Recipes I've Made

Desserts!

Raspberry Lemon Scones

Rating: 3/5

I made these scones with my mom over winter break, when
we were looking for a fun thing to eat with our tea (as aspiring
Irish residents do. I wasn't a big fan of the lemon flavor, but
these were overall a fun way to spice up teatime.

White Chocolate Chai Pumpkin Snickerdoodles

Rating: 5/5

These are arguably the perfect cookies. Unless you're my
brother who is allergic to chai spice. But seriously, I know
you're judging the white chocolate. And I am a white chocolate fan.
However, my friends who have been staunchly against white chocolate
in the past LOVED these cookies. So ha!

Entrees!

Pad Thai Spring Rolls

Rating: 4/5

Another endeavour made with the help of my mom, since this
one was a bit labor-intensive. Lots of chopping, shredding, noodle
cooking, and wrapping involved. Not to mention the peanut sauce,
which will make an appearance on this page below. But it was worth
it for a fun spin on an old classic.

Asado Chicken Breast with Sauteed Lemon Zucchini

Rating: 3/5

As a zucchini fan, I had such high hopes for this recipe! Note:
I substituted chicken breast for chicken tenders because I have a deep-
rooted fear of cooking meat unless it is in very small sizes. Anyway,
something about cooking the zucchini in the leftover chicken goop irked
me. And as with the scones, I'm not a big lemon fan.

Soups!

Creamy Tuscan Garlic Tortellini Soup

Rating: 4.5/5

The perfect winter soup. Perfect to make in huge quantities so you
can eat it for the next five days. I went ham on this soup and kind of
changed the recipe to whatever I wanted, adding mushrooms and chickpeas.
It really made me feel like a chef and the whole experience was lovely.

Breakfast!

Frozen Yogurt Covered Blueberries

Rating: 3.5/5

If you can't enjoy just putting frozen blueberries into your cup
of yogurt, this is the dish for you. Although delicious, I spent a ton
of time covering blueberries in yogurt and letting them freeze on a tray.
However, they were pretty delicious, so I might try this one again anyway.

Top 3

  1. White Chocolate Chai Pumpkin Snickerdoodles
  2. Creamy Tuscan Garlic Tortellini Soup
  3. Pad Thai Spring Rolls
