Songs I'm obsessed with right now in no particular order

I Bet You Think About Me (feat. Chris Stapleton)

Taylor Swift, Red (Taylor's Version)

  • Such a fun and unique asset to the Red (Taylor's Version),
    I Bet You Think About Me leaves you reminiscing on Swift's twangy
    country origins and cursing the name of Jake Gyllenhaal.

Love To Keep Me Warm

dodie, Laufey, Love To Keep Me Warm

  • Still lingering in my mind from Christmas, this new release from
    Youtube star-turned indie artist dodie and Icelandic-Chinese TikTok
    star Laufey feels like the newest Christmas classic to me.

cowboy like me

Taylor Swift, evermore

  • One of Swift's more genius pieces of music, lyrically speaking.
    The artistry in cowboy like me is evidence of Swift's growth as an artist.
    This one's perfect to add to your "Westworld vibes" playlist.

It's Called: Freefall

Rainbow Kitten Surprise, How To: Friend, Love, Freefall

  • Yes, I heard this one on TikTok first. However, this song will make you
    feel like the good guy who's just decided to become the villain. But like,
    in a cool, subtle way.

A Burning Hill

Mitski, Puberty 2

  • Just devastating. Mitski really knows how to pack a punch in a song that's
    only one minute and fifty seconds long. It's simplistic, but the imagery
    is just stunning. Don't listen to this one on a bad day.

Mushaboom

Feist, Let It Die

  • Just great vibes. Listen to this and then try and tell me you weren't
    tapping your foot or bobbing your head. This song came to me when
    exploring friends' playlists, and it hasn't let me down since.

Death With Dignity

Sufjan Stevens, Carrie & Lowell

  • Stevens has done it again. Transpoerted me to an alternate universe
    where fairies are real and I can walk around in the forest all day
    collecting pretty rocks. Also, can you say devastating lyrics? Mitski
    has some competition here.
