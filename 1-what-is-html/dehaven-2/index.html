I used to hate working out. Now, I schedule my classes around my gym time.

Finding time to work out is hard. Finding the energy to work out is hard. McComas group exercise has given me a way to stay motivated and fit in a hectic post-pandemic world.

In case you care to join me, here's my average workout schedule for the Spring 2022 semester.

Bodypump

>

I do Bodypump45 (or the full hour, depending on my energy level,) on these days:

  • Wednesday's at 4:00
  • Friday's at 3:30
  • Saturday's at 11:00
  • Sunday's at 12:30

Cycle

I do Cycle for 45 minutes on these days:

  • Monday's at 5:15
  • Tuesday's at 5:15
  • Thursday's at 5:15

Other Workouts

Sometimes, my schedule doesn't permit me to go to the classes that I want to. Life happens. It's not ideal, but it's realistic. On those days, I'll ususally:

  • Run on the treadmill for 2.5-3 miles
  • Bike for 15-18 miles
  • Weightlift (and hope no man tries to correct my form)
