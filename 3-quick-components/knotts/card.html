How to Play Cards

Go Fish

Directions

To play Go Fish, you have to meet a few requirements:

  • Minimum of two players
  • A deck of cards
  • Ability to read numbers

Rules

There are a few rules regarding Go Fish.

  • You can only draw one card at a time
  • Don't look at your players cards
  • Use the phrase Go Fish when the opponent guesses wrong
