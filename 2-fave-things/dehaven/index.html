Zenia's Favorite Things

Everybody has their favorites. Favorite movies, TV shows, ice cream, comic book superhero, books, etcs. Here's some fun facts about my favorite things.

Video Games

  • Pokemon

    • I've been in love with these games for as long as I can remember. Though the new games have their flaws (being too easy, having questionable new designs, not enough banging music,) I love the series too much to complain. You get to collect a bunch of animal friends and make them fight each other. What more could you want?

  • Stardew Valley

    • This is a game I recommend to everyone regardless of previous gaming experience. You could have 10,000 hours on GTA or have never touched a console since your Nintendo DS and I would still recommend trying this game. There's combat with underground monsters, romancing options, and, most importantly, a fishing mini game.
